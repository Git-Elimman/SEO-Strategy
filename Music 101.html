WHAT IS MUSIC?

Music is simply the combination of organised sounds that are pleasant to the ears.

Sounds that are not organised are regarded as noise, thus, it is important to note that the sounds must be organised before it can be termed music. Music has been in existence for thousands and millions of year before you and me and has evolved over time.

Staff

Staff is a set of five (5) horizontal lines and four spaces used in writing sheet music. The staff begins with a symbol known as a clef, which most times is either a treble clef or bass clef. The lines and spaces of the staff, also known as stave have names which are: E F G A B C D E F

Accidentals

Accidentals are signs that increase or reduce the quality of the affecteed note by a tone or semitone. Commonly used accidentals are:

Sharp
Increases the quality of a note by a semitone.
Flat
Reduces the quality of a note by a semitone.
Double Sharp
Increases the quality of a note by a tone.
Double Flat
Reduces the quality of a note by a tone.
